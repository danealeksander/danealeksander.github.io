Dane is a graphic artist in Halifax, Canada—in Mi’kma’ki, the ancestral and unceded territory of the Mi’kmaq People—in service of visual art and visual identity design for wild projects on Turtle Island and around the world. Dane is also a technical animation artist licensed with Autodesk Maya, specialized in the production of creature visual effects art for interactive media and motion pictures. Dane is available for art commissions in 2026.
