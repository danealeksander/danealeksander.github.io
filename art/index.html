“My passion for art is inspired by nature. Color and light in nature. Unity and variety in nature. Most of all the wildlife—the wild places and the animals that live in them, knowing that most of us will never experience many endangered species as anything more than fantastic ideas in a story. The wild ones are figments of our shared imagination.”


      


      

        
		    
Dane Aleksander, Washington DC, 2013


      


    


  


  

    

      
Dane is a wildlife painter in Canada, inspired to illustrate the wild places and the animals that live in them for picture books and story art and so on.
